Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MOFx Expert Advisors

What is an Expert Advisor (EA)? ‚ñº
An Expert Advisor is an automated trading program that runs on MetaTrader platforms (MT4/MT5). It analyzes market conditions and executes trades based on predefined algorithms, allowing you to trade 24/7 without constant monitoring.
How do MOFx EAs work? ‚ñº
MOFx EAs use advanced algorithms to identify trading opportunities based on technical indicators, price action, and market conditions. Each EA is designed for specific strategies and includes risk management features like stop-loss, take-profit, and position sizing controls.
Which brokers are compatible with MOFx EAs? ‚ñº
Our EAs work with any broker that supports MetaTrader 4 or MetaTrader 5. We recommend our partner brokers (Exness, Vantage, FTMO, BlueGuardian) for optimal performance, low spreads, and reliable execution.
Do I need a VPS to run EAs? ‚ñº
While not mandatory, a VPS (Virtual Private Server) is highly recommended for EA trading. It ensures 24/7 uptime, eliminates internet connectivity issues, provides low latency connection to broker servers, and prevents missed trades due to computer downtime.
What are the risks of automated trading? ‚ñº
Automated trading carries the same risks as manual trading, including potential loss of capital. Market conditions can change rapidly, and past performance does not guarantee future results. Always start with proper risk management, use appropriate position sizing, and never risk more than you can afford to lose.
Do you provide support and updates? ‚ñº
Yes, we provide comprehensive support via WhatsApp, Telegram, and email. All EAs include free updates to adapt to changing market conditions. Premium tutorial subscribers also receive priority support and personalized guidance.
Can I customize EA settings? ‚ñº
Absolutely. All MOFx EAs come with adjustable parameters including lot size, risk percentage, take-profit levels, stop-loss settings, and trading hours. We provide detailed documentation and premium tutorials on optimal configuration.
How do I get started? ‚ñº
Getting started is simple: choose your EA, complete payment via Telegram or credit card (Paystack), receive your EA files and installation guide, install on MT4/MT5, configure settings based on your risk tolerance, and start trading. We provide step-by-step guidance throughout the process.
